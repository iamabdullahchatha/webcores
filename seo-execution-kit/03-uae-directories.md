# 03 — UAE Directories

Local UAE-specific directories are weighted higher by Google for queries
with a UAE geo qualifier. A Connect.ae or HiDubai listing beats another
global Web 2.0 directory for "Dubai SEO agency" type searches.

All 8 below are free (or have a free tier that's sufficient). Skip the
upsells.

## Per-directory specifics

### 1. Connect.ae

The single most-trafficked UAE business directory. Worth doing first.

- **Category**: `IT Services > Web Development`
- **Secondary**: `IT Services > Software`, `Marketing > SEO`, `Design > Graphic Design`
- **Description**: **Mid bio**
- **Photos**: upload 3-5 (logo, team, sample work)
- **Hours**: as in `01-company-data.md`

A free basic listing is sufficient. The paid tiers offer featured placement
inside Connect.ae's own search but don't change SEO benefit. Skip the paid
options initially.

---

### 2. Yellow Pages UAE

Old-school but still well-indexed by Google.

- **Category**: `Computer & Internet Services > Web Design`
- **Description**: **Short bio**

---

### 3. HiDubai

One of the most popular UAE business directories. Photos help internal
ranking.

- **Category**: `Computer & IT > Web Design`
- **Subcategories**: up to 3 — pick `Software Development`, `SEO`, `Graphic Design`
- **Description**: **Mid bio**
- **Photos**: 5+ (HiDubai listings with photos rank higher in their own
  internal search, which drives some referral traffic)

---

### 4. Dubai Business Directory

Dubai-focused, helps for "agency Dubai" queries.

- **Category**: `Information Technology`
- **Description**: **Mid bio**

---

### 5. Yelo.ae

Growing UAE local-search engine. Smaller authority than HiDubai but worth
the 15 minutes.

- **Category**: `Business Services > IT & Software`
- **Description**: **Short bio**

---

### 6. UAE Business Pages

Older directory, still indexed.

- **Category**: `Computer Services`
- **Description**: **Short bio**

---

### 7. Gulf Business Directory

Broader GCC reach (covers Saudi, Kuwait, Bahrain) — useful when you target
GCC clients beyond the UAE.

- **Category**: `IT & Software`
- **Description**: **Mid bio**

---

### 8. TimeOut Dubai Business

Not a directory in the traditional sense — TimeOut Dubai is editorial.
Submit a pitch via their contact form rather than a directory listing.

Pitch template:

```
Subject: Dubai tech founder pitch — Webcore Solutions

Hi TimeOut Dubai team,

I'm Muhammad Abdullah Chattha, founder of Webcore Solutions, a Dubai
digital agency now in its 13th year (founded 2012). We grew from a
3-person team to a 25-person agency without external funding, serving
UAE businesses and global brands.

A few angles that may suit TimeOut Dubai's business or tech coverage:

1. Bootstrapped Dubai tech founders — how we grew without VC
2. The rise of AI-assisted design in Dubai creative agencies
3. Why UAE SMEs are insourcing software development in 2026

Happy to share data and on-the-record quotes. I can also offer one of
our designers or engineers for a follow-up interview.

Best,
Muhammad Abdullah Chattha
Webcore Solutions
+971 50 716 9200
info@webcoreuae.com
https://www.webcoreuae.com
```

Realistic outcome: 1 in 5 pitches gets a reply, ~1 in 10 gets a placement.
Worth the 20 minutes.
