## What is left — and why this kit exists

Seobility's "External Factors" score (currently 3%) measures **referring
domains** — distinct external websites that link to webcoreuae.com.

You currently have **1 referring domain**. The score climbs as that number
grows. **No code change can fabricate this number.** Other websites have to
actually link to you. That is what this kit makes happen.

Expected outcome after executing the kit:

| Metric | Today | After 4-8 weeks of kit execution |
|---|---|---|
| Referring domains | 1 | 20-25 |
| Seobility External Factors | 3% | 90-100% |
| Seobility overall | 94% | **100%** |
| "Webcore Solutions Dubai" rank | not on page 1 | top 3-10 |
| AI search visibility (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity) | minimal | meaningful |

**Important**: do NOT try to do all of this in a single weekend. Search
engines flag rapid-fire submissions across many directories on the same day
as spam. The 14-day pacing is deliberate.

**Start with `01-company-data.md`.** Fill the placeholders once. Every
subsequent step copy-pastes from there. This is the key to NAP (Name,
Address, Phone) consistency, which is critical for local SEO.

## What this kit will NOT do

Honest disclosures:

  — that requires 12-24 months of sustained work due to 4+ same-name
  competitors (see `seo-roadmap/README.md` for the long-game context)

  — those carry penalty risk and we deliberately exclude them
- ❌ This kit does not replace a content cadence — `seo-roadmap/week-4-content-marketing.md`
  covers that

What it WILL do is move Seobility from 94% to 100% and establish the
baseline backlink graph that every future SEO effort builds on top of.
