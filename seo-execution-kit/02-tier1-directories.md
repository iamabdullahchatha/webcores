## Per-directory specifics

### 1. Google Business Profile  ⭐ MOST IMPORTANT

This single listing affects Maps, Local Pack, Knowledge Panel, and the
review ecosystem. Do this first.

- **Category (primary)**: `Website designer`
- **Categories (secondary)**: `Software company`, `Internet marketing service`,
  `Graphic designer`
- **Service area**: Dubai, UAE (mark as a service-area business if you do
  not host visitors at the office)
- **Hours**: use the hours block from `01-company-data.md`
- **Description**: paste **Mid bio**
- **Verification**: choose postcard, phone, or video — postcard is most
  reliable for UAE addresses
- **First-day actions after verification**: upload 5 photos (logo, team,
  workspace, sample work, founder), post one short "Welcome" update

Expected outcome: appears in Google Maps search for "Webcore Solutions Dubai"
within 7-14 days. Knowledge Panel signal builds over 4-8 weeks.

---

### 2. Bing Places

Bing Places powers DuckDuckGo, ChatGPT Search, and Bing Copilot — about
6-8% of UAE search volume by mid-2026. Worth the 20 minutes.

- **Category**: `Web designer` → add `Software company`, `SEO services`
- **Hours**: as in `01-company-data.md`
- **Description**: **Mid bio**
- **Verification**: postcard or phone

If you already verified Google Business, Bing can sometimes import the
data — look for the "import from Google" option to save 10 minutes.

---

### 3. LinkedIn Company Page

- **URL slug**: claim `linkedin.com/company/webcore-solutions-uae` (the
  `-uae` suffix is critical for entity disambiguation)
- **Industry**: `Software Development`
- **Company size**: `11-50 employees`
- **Company type**: `Privately Held`
- **Founded**: `2012`
- **Tagline**: paste **Tagline** (Bio Version 1)
- **About**: paste **Long bio** (Bio Version 4)
- **Logo**: 300×300 minimum
- **Cover image**: 1128×191 (create from existing brand assets)

After page creation, publish 1 short welcome post. Pin it. This makes the
page look active to recruiters and prospects.

---

### 4. Clutch.co

Clutch profiles rank highly for "best [service] agency Dubai" queries
because Clutch itself has DR ~90.

- **Service Lines**: Web Development, UX/UI Design, SEO, Custom Software
  Development, Search Engine Optimization
- **Industries served**: pick the 5 your client base actually covers
- **Hourly rate range**: `$50 – $99 / hr`
- **Minimum project size**: `$5,000+`
- **Portfolio**: add at least 3 client projects with measurable outcomes
  (use anonymised metrics if NDA prevents naming clients)
- **Description**: **Long bio**

Critical: after the listing is live, request 3-5 client reviews via
Clutch's official process. Clutch reviews are phone-verified and far
more valuable than directory listings alone.

and slow approval (some directory teams deprioritise impatient
submitters).

Move to `04-social-profiles.md` for days 5-7 work while these
approvals percolate.
