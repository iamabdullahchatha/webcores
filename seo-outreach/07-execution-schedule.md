# 21-Day Backlink Acquisition Schedule

> **Target:** 20+ referring domains in 21 days.
> **Pace:** 3–5 actions per day maximum. Rapid bulk submissions look spammy and can trigger manual review flags on some directories.
> **Time per day:** Estimate 30–60 minutes daily.
