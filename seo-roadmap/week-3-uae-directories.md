# Week 3 — UAE Directory Submissions (User Executes)

UAE-specific directories matter more than global ones for local SEO. A
listing on Connect.ae or HiDubai is a stronger Dubai-context signal than
another global directory entry. Several of these accept submissions for
free; a few have paid tiers — paid is **not required** for the SEO benefit.

**Time required**: 4-6 hours total.

Reuse the bios and NAP from `week-2-backlinks-tier-1.md`. Consistency is
critical — Google compares your NAP across all listings.

---

## 1. Connect.ae

- **URL**: https://www.connect.ae/business-signup
- **Plan**: Free listing exists; paid tiers exist (skip them initially)
- **Approval**: 3-7 days
- **Notes**: Strongest UAE-only signal. Worth doing first.

**Categories**: `IT Services > Web Development`, `IT Services > Software`,
`Marketing > SEO`, `Design > Graphic Design`.

## 4. HiDubai

- **URL**: https://www.hidubai.com/login (then "Add your business")
- **Plan**: Free basic profile
- **Approval**: 3-5 days
- **Notes**: One of the most-trafficked UAE business directories.

**Specifics for HiDubai**:
- Choose category: `Computer & IT > Web Design`
- Sub-categories: add up to 3 (`Software Development`, `SEO`, `Graphic Design`)
- Add 5+ photos (HiDubai listings with photos rank higher in their internal search)

## 9. TimeOut Dubai Business

- **URL**: https://www.timeoutdubai.com/aboutus (contact form route)
- **Plan**: Inclusion requires editorial pitch, not a directory submission
- **Approval**: Variable; usually 2-4 weeks
- **Notes**: This is more of a PR ask than a directory. Pitch your founder
  for a "Dubai Tech Founders to Watch" angle. Template:

```

Happy to share data, customer stories, and on-the-record quotes for any
```

Once all directory submissions are pending, do nothing for ~10 days. They
will trickle in. Resist the urge to chase status updates daily — that
wastes the user's time. Move on to Week 4 content marketing while these
listings approve in the background.
