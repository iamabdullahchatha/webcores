## 1. Google Business Profile (THE most important one)

- **URL**: https://business.google.com/create
- **Time**: 30-45 minutes (verification can take days)
- **Approval**: Google posts a verification card or video call within 5-14 days
- **Priority**: Critical — controls local Knowledge Panel + Maps + Reviews

**Steps**:
1. Sign in with the Google account that will manage the listing
2. Business name: `Webcore Solutions`
3. Category: `Website designer` (primary), add: `Software company`, `Internet marketing service`, `Graphic designer`
4. Service area / address: Dubai, UAE (mark as service-area business if no public office)
5. Phone: `+971 50 716 9200`
6. Website: `https://www.webcoreuae.com`
7. Description: paste the mid bio above
8. Add 5+ photos (logo, team, office or work samples)
9. Choose verification method (postcard / phone / video)

**After verification**:
- Post weekly updates (Google rewards activity)
- Reply to every review within 48 hours
- Add Service entries for each of the 6 service categories with descriptions

[ ] Submitted   [ ] Verified   [ ] First 5 photos uploaded

## 4. Clutch.co

- **URL**: https://clutch.co/profile/get-listed/new
- **Time**: 30-45 minutes
- **Approval**: 5-10 business days; analyst may call
- **Priority**: Very high — Clutch listings rank well for "best [X] agency Dubai"

**Steps**:
1. Create company account at the URL above
2. Paste long bio in the company overview
3. Add Service Lines (Clutch's taxonomy):
   - Web Development
   - Mobile App Development (if applicable, else skip)
   - UX/UI Design
   - Search Engine Optimization
   - Custom Software Development
4. Add at least 3 client projects with metrics (use existing testimonial data)
5. Hourly rate range: `$50-$99 / hr` (adjust to actual)
6. Minimum project size: `$5,000+`
7. Office location: Dubai, UAE

**After listing is live**: request reviews from 3-5 existing clients
(template in week-7-8-social-proof.md). Clutch reviews are verified by phone,
so warn clients to expect a call.
