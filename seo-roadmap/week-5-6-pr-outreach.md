# Weeks 5-6 — PR & Guest Posting (User Executes)

By week 5, the directory backlinks from weeks 2-3 are starting to index. The
next signal Google looks for is **editorial backlinks** — links from real
publications that wrote about you, not directory entries.

These are the hardest to get. They are also the most valuable per-link.

**Time required**: 4-6 hours per week for 2 weeks. Most of that is writing
follow-ups and tweaking pitches.

**Realistic outcome**: Out of 15 outlets pitched, expect 1-3 to respond
positively. That is normal. Pitching at scale is the only way to get even
one good placement.

## Pitch template A — "Founder story" angle (most common)

Subject line:
```
Pitch — How a Dubai bootstrapped agency hit 450 clients in 12 years
```

Body:
```
Hi [Editor first name],

I'm Muhammad Abdullah Chattha, founder of Webcore Solutions, a Dubai-based
digital agency now in its 13th year. We crossed 450 client projects last
quarter and grew to 25 full-time team members — all without external
funding, all serving clients across the UAE, the UK, the US, and Pakistan.

A few angles that might fit [Publication name]'s coverage:

1. The economics of a bootstrapped Dubai agency in 2026 — why some
   founders are deliberately staying off VC and what it costs
2. How AI-assisted design and engineering changed our delivery model in
   the last 18 months (with data on team productivity)
3. Why mid-market UAE businesses are insourcing software development
   teams instead of hiring agencies — and how agencies are adapting

Happy to send a longer write-up on any of these, share supporting data,
and provide a quote on the record. I can also offer an interview slot
this or next week if useful.

Best,
Muhammad Abdullah Chattha
Founder, Webcore Solutions

muhammad@webcoreuae.com [replace with actual founder email]
https://www.webcoreuae.com
```

## Realistic outcome by end of week 6

- 15 pitches sent, ~3-5 replies, 1-2 confirmed placements
- 1 guest post in progress for publication ~4-6 weeks out
- 1 short quote / commentary placed in a business news piece
- 0-1 founder-story article being scheduled

This is success. Anyone telling you to expect 10 placements from 15
pitches is selling something.
